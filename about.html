WE MADE IT EASY

This port was brought to you after weeks of work.
It includes an easy-to-use update process.
For that, we had to prepare special cabs called "Features cabs"
which ensure you have a functioning build and give you the ability to use your unlocked phone as much as possible.
It also involved major updates for Universal Update 2.7.1.

NON-STOP FLASHING

This wasn't possible without the team efforts of the WindowsPhone community developers,
and we had to make endless flashing for our phones to make this as easy as possible.

We hope you will enjoy it.

WHAT'S NEW
